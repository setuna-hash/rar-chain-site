About

Built for autonomous value transfer.

RAR Chain begins as a payment infrastructure for autonomous AI agents. Its initial role is to support machine-to-machine micro-payments, especially for data access and computational resources, while building toward a broader decentralized AI economy.

AI Agent Payments

RAR is designed to enable AI agents to pay for services, data, and external resources without relying on traditional financial rails.

Scarcity Model

The network follows a burn-linked token model designed to support long-term scarcity and sustainable incentives.

Decentralized Direction

RAR Chain is built with a long-term vision of distributed governance, resilient infrastructure, and an open financial layer for the AI era.

Roadmap

Project Summary

RAR Chain is an experimental decentralized financial infrastructure project designed for the AI era.

The project explores new models of token scarcity, machine-to-machine payments, distributed governance, and network sustainability.
